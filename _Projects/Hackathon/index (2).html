EMU Carpool Tool

Solution to the EMU Parking Problem

The Problem

Parking Lots Are Full

The increasing size of the student population has caused the parking lots to become full.

The Solution

Carpooling

By creating incentives for carpooling and making it easier for students to setup carpools with fellow students, we can reduce the number of cars in the parking lots. Say goodbye to the "Car Sharks."





Group 7, EMU Hackathon 2015
